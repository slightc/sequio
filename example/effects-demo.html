Effects & Transitions playground (milestone 07)

Left: a clip carrying a ColorEffect + BlurEffect — drag the sliders to drive their animatable params (the canvas repaints on each change; the SDK never auto-repaints). Tick Animate to keyframe them and let the render clock sweep updateAt(t). Tick Global to move the grade onto Compositor.effects — it then also hits the teal badge (which has no effect of its own), proving it applies to the whole composite. Middle: two clips A/B overlap on one track with a CrossfadeTransition bound via track.addTransition(tr.between(A, B)) — scrub the timeline (crossfade happens over the overlap 1–2s) or press Play. Right: geometric warps — perspective corner-pin, bulge/pinch, or a displacement-map ripple (things Transform2D's affine transform can't do). Pick a type and drag the amount.
